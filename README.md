## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### 🐞 I found a bug
Please email us with:
- Device model  
- iOS version  
- Description of the issue  

Screenshots are very helpful if you can include them.

---

### 💾 I lost my progress
If your progress was linked to your Apple ID, try:
1. Reinstalling the app  
2. Restoring purchases (if applicable)  

If the issue persists, contact us.
